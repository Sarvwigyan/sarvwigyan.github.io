Privacy Policy for Sarvwigyan

Welcome to Sarvwigyan. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use any of our services, including:

  • Sarvwigyan (Main Platform) – Knowledge hub, articles, books, tools.
  • SarvStore – Digital store for books, resources, products.
  • SarvPedia – Encyclopedia-style knowledge platform.
  • Any other apps, websites, or tools developed under the Sarvwigyan Ecosystem.

1. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide Directly

  • Name
  • Email Address
  • Account Credentials (for login)
  • Feedback, form submissions, comments
  • Any data you upload or store in our services

B. Information We Collect Automatically

  • Device information (browser, OS, device type)
  • IP Address (for security & analytics)
  • Usage patterns (pages visited, clicks, actions)
  • Cookies and local storage data

C. Third‑Party Data

If you sign in using Google or any third‑party service, we may receive basic profile information they provide.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account across all Sarvwigyan platforms (Single Unified Login)
  • To personalize your experience and content
  • To recommend books, posts, tools and resources
  • To improve our services and user interface
  • To store your preferences, history, and saved items from SarvStore, SarvPedia, etc.
  • To ensure security and prevent misuse

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal data.

Your data may be shared with:

  • Our internal Sarvwigyan services (SarvStore, SarvPedia, AI tools)
  • Trusted third‑party tools used for hosting, authentication or analytics (e.g., Firebase)
  • Legal authorities if required by law

4. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies, local storage, and session storage to:

  • Keep you signed in
  • Save your preferences
  • Track usage to improve the platform

5. Data Security

We use standard industry methods like HTTPS, encryption where needed, and secure authentication (e.g., Firebase Auth) to protect your data.

6. Your Rights

  • Access your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Change or update your account information
  • Opt out of analytics or tracking cookies

7. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you think such data exists, contact us and we will remove it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Any major changes will be shown on this page.
