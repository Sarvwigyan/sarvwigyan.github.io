Argon (Ar)

Basic Properties

Atomic Number: 18

Atomic Mass: 39.948 u

Density: 1.784 g/L

Melting Point: -189.3°C

Boiling Point: -185.8°C

Electronic Configuration: [Ne] 3s² 3p⁶

Phase at STP: Gas

Color: Colorless

Odor: Odorless

Isotopes: ³⁶Ar, ³⁸Ar, ⁴⁰Ar (Stable)

Ionization Energy: 1520.6 kJ/mol

Van der Waals Radius: 188 pm

Thermal Conductivity: 17.72 mW/(m·K)

Discovery: 1894 by Lord Rayleigh and William Ramsay

Abundance in Earth's Atmosphere: 0.934%

Reactivity: Inert (Noble Gas)

Common Uses: Lighting, Welding, Cryogenics
